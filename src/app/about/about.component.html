Paul Saltanis has over 40 years of experience dealing with outdoor pests. With four CT DEEP licenses and a Northeast Organic Land Care Accreditation, he has helped thousands of homeowners (along with several municipalities) to control their ticks and mosquitoes.

Paul is deeply concerned about the environment. He understands the impact that pesticides can have, so he uses nothing but the safest possible products.

Country Tick & Mosquito is licensed to apply both organic and pesticide–based products to control ticks and mosquitoes, depending on your individual needs and preferences.

We recommend our organic, pesticide–free treatments for those with small children, outdoor pets, or vegetable gardens. We use a combination of peppermint and cedar oils that kills ticks and mosquitoes on contact.

If your property is near wooded areas or wetlands, or has a lot of wildlife such as deer, then we would suggest a pesticide–based program that would provide some residual control to keep ticks and mosquitoes from coming back.

We have the equipment to do the job right! We have a large truck mounted spray system with two tanks. This gives us the option of using a variety of products. The high-power spray system provides superior coverage and penetrates the places these pests hide.
